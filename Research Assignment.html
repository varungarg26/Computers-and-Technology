Computing and The Environment Ethical Use of Computers Career Oppurtunities
Although the use of computers has many drawbacks with the impacts of it on the environment, it also has very good impacts, and makes the earth very environmentally friendly.
  • It helps us develop and produce new materials and are sustainable and do not harm the environment, unlike those that do.
  • It allows us to monitor the environment and make future predictions about it, which help us understand it, better.
  • It helps us create smarter technologies that save energy and keep the environment, such as motion sensor lights, which only turn on when there is motion, and automatically turn off when there is no one in the room.
  • People from all over the world can interact, and collaborate, and officials and share their research, have online meeting, hence reducing the cost, and pollution caused by travelling to other places.
  • It allows for paperless communication, such as email, and paperless billing, therefore reducing the amount of trees that are cut down.
  • It relieves the effort of travelling to stores to purchase materials, and people can purchase things online, and companies and industries and ship materials all over the world.

People nowadays become very excited when purchasing and using newer technology, and do not think about the negative impacts that it is having on the environment. Therefore it is necessary that we consider these negative impacts and use gadgets in the safest and most responsible way possible. So that we can solve problems, not create more problems for the near future.
